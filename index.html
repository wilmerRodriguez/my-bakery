About me

A lifelong love

Hello! I'm a passionate baker based in Paris, dedicated to creating handcrafted treats that bring people together. From flaky croissants to celebration cakes, every item is made with care and the finest local ingredients.

I believe baking is both an art and a science — a precise craft that, when done right, creates moments of pure joy. Whether it's a wedding cake or a simple sourdough loaf, I pour the same dedication into every creation.
